Overview

3-Amino-2-chloro-6-methylphenol is a multifunctional aromatic intermediate combining amino, chloro and methyl substituents on a phenolic core. It is used primarily in colorant chemistry

Synonyms

  • 3-amino-2-chloro-6-methylphenol
  • 2-chloro-6-methyl-3-aminophenol

Applications

  • Hair dye intermediates and oxidative colorant chemistry
  • Specialty dye and pigment synthesis
  • Research reagent for synthesis of substituted phenols
