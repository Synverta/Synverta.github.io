3-Amino-2-chloro-6-methylphenol

A substituted aminophenol used as an intermediate in dye and hair-color chemistry, and in certain specialty organic syntheses.

Overview

3-Amino-2-chloro-6-methylphenol is a multifunctional aromatic intermediate combining amino, chloro and methyl substituents on a phenolic core. It is used primarily in colorant chemistry and as a reactive intermediate for specialty formulations.

Synonyms

  • 3-amino-2-chloro-6-methylphenol
  • 2-chloro-6-methyl-3-aminophenol

Applications

  • Hair dye intermediates and oxidative colorant chemistry
  • Specialty dye and pigment synthesis
  • Research reagent for synthesis of substituted phenols

Physical & Chemical Properties

  • Appearance: off-white to light yellow crystalline solid
  • Chemical formula: C7H8ClNO
  • Molecular weight: ~165.59 g·mol⁻¹
  • Solubility: moderate in polar organic solvents; limited in water

Handling & Safety

This substituted aminophenol may be an irritant and sensitizer. Use appropriate protective equipment and avoid environmental release.

  • Wear gloves, goggles and protective clothing; avoid dust and vapors.
  • Handle in well-ventilated area; use fume hood if necessary.
  • Refer to supplier SDS for detailed emergency measures and disposal guidance.

Packaging & Storage

  • Packaging: small glass bottles for laboratory quantities; drums/bags for industrial orders.
  • Storage: cool, dry place in tightly sealed containers, away from strong oxidizers and acids.
