2-Methylresorcinol

An aromatic dihydroxy compound used as an intermediate for dyes, pigments, cosmetics and certain pharmaceutical syntheses.

Overview

2-Methylresorcinol (2-methyl-1,3-benzenediol) is an aromatic building block featuring two phenolic OH groups and a methyl substituent. It is supplied as a crystalline solid and is used primarily as a synthetic intermediate for hair dye components, specialty dyes, pigment precursors, and in research chemistry.

Common Synonyms

  • 2-Methyl-1,3-benzenediol
  • 1,3-Dihydroxy-2-methylbenzene
  • 2,6-Dihydroxytoluene (less common)

Typical Applications

  • Intermediate for dyes and pigments
  • Oxidative and non-oxidative hair dye formulations
  • Pharmaceutical and cosmetic intermediates
  • Research reagent for organic synthesis

Handling & Safety

2-Methylresorcinol can be an irritant and has toxicity concerns in higher exposures; handle with normal chemical safety precautions:

  • Use appropriate PPE: gloves, eye protection and lab coat; avoid skin contact.
  • Avoid inhalation of dust; use local exhaust or respiratory protection if dust may be generated.
  • Work in a well-ventilated area; prevent release to the environment.
  • Consult supplier SDS for full hazard statements and first-aid measures.

Packaging & Storage

  • Typical packaging: small glass bottles, HDPE bottles (25 g, 100 g, 250 g) and larger containers available on request.
  • Storage: keep tightly closed in a cool, dry, well-ventilated place; protect from strong oxidizers and heat.
  • Shelf life: stable under recommended storage conditions; refer to supplier for batch-specific guidance.
