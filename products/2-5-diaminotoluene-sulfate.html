2,5-Diaminotoluene sulfate

A salt form of 2,5-diaminotoluene used predominantly in hair-dye intermediates and specialty chemical synthesis; provided as a crystalline sulfate salt for improved handling and solubility in aqueous formulations.

Overview

2,5-Diaminotoluene sulfate is the sulfate salt of the aromatic diamine 2,5-diaminotoluene. The sulfate form improves water compatibility and handling compared with the free base. It is widely used as an intermediate in oxidative hair dye formulations and as a building block for dyes, pigments and specialty organics.

Common Synonyms

  • 2,5-Diaminotoluene sulfate
  • Toluene-2,5-diamine sulfate
  • 2,5-Toluenediamine sulfate

Typical Applications

  • Oxidative hair dye intermediates (regulated cosmetic use)
  • Intermediate for azo and direct dye synthesis
  • Specialty pigment and reagent manufacture
  • Research and development in colorant chemistry

  • Moisture/content and assay: per supplier Certificate of Analysis

Handling & Safety

As an aromatic diamine salt, it can cause skin and eye irritation and may be a sensitizer. Follow precautions below:

  • Use gloves, eye protection and protective clothing; avoid inhalation of dust.
  • Handle in well-ventilated areas or fume hood.
  • In case of contact, flush with water and seek medical advice for severe effects.
  • Dispose of waste per local hazardous waste regulations; avoid environmental release.

Packaging & Storage

  • Packaging: sealed plastic or foil-lined bags, drums or bottles depending on quantity.
  • Storage: cool, dry, well-ventilated area away from strong oxidizing agents and acids; keep tightly closed.
